About

မင်္ဂလာပါ. வனக்கம். Hello.

My name is Raphael Ram, and I’m a writer and web developer based in New York City.

When I was a teenager in Myanmar, my uncle lent me his install CD for Ubuntu 10.04. It was love at first install, and Linux and Open Source software have since led me to pursue a career in tech, with a recent shift in focus to Web Development.

Aside from coding, I enjoy immersing myself in music, especially through audiophile headphones, and getting my soul’s fill of beautiful film and anime. You can read my thoughts about these and other things on my personal blog (coming soon to a browser near you).
